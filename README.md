# 📊 HR Resignation Data Analysis

> Internship Project by **Mst. Mafroza Haque Iqra**  
> IT Division, ACME Laboratories Ltd. | Summer 2024–2025

---

## 📌 Overview
A **data analysis project** developed for the HR Department of ACME Laboratories Ltd. to identify employee resignation patterns — which departments have the highest turnover, which months see the most resignations, and what the top reasons are. The findings were presented to management to support future employee retention strategy planning.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify which departments have the **highest resignation rates**
- Discover **peak resignation months** throughout the year
- Find the **top 10 reasons** employees leave the organization
- Provide **visual reports** to help management make data-driven HR decisions

---

## ✨ Features

### 🧹 Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Cleaned raw resignation dataset
- Fixed inconsistent and missing values
- Transformed data into analysis-ready format using Pandas

### 🔍 Analysis Performed
- Monthly resignation count over time
- Department-wise resignation breakdown
- Job title-wise resignation breakdown
- Top 10 resignation reasons identified and ranked

### 📊 Visualizations
- **Department-wise Bar Chart** — horizontal bar chart showing total resignations per department
- **Top 10 Resignation Reasons** — bar chart of most frequently cited resignation reasons
- **Monthly Resignation Trend** — line graph showing how resignations changed month by month
- **Management Reports** — pie charts, line graphs, and bar graphs prepared for management meetings

### 📈 Key Finding
> 💡 **Quality Operation (SDU, BLS, HSU)** had the highest number of resignations — close to **120 cases**, making it the most critical department for HR intervention.
